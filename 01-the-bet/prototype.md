# The Prototype Bet

## What I Built

A working prototype of GridMind's core workflow: a developer inputs a renewable energy project (location, technology type, MW capacity) and the AI returns a permitting risk assessment — predicted blockers, recommended approval path, and a pre-drafted RFI response template for the most likely objection.

## AI Value Archetype

**Oracle + Automator**  
Oracle: predicts approval blockers and timelines before submission, reducing expensive surprises.  
Automator: generates regulatory filings, RFI responses, and study request documentation automatically, eliminating weeks of manual drafting.

## The Bet in One Sentence

GridMind turns the single biggest bottleneck in the renewable energy transition — regulatory permitting — from a 7-year guessing game into a data-driven, AI-navigated process, for the project developers who have $1.4T in capital waiting to connect to the grid.

## Kill Criteria

Stop if:
- Fewer than 3 of 10 pilot developers say the predicted blockers match their actual experience (accuracy signal is too weak to build a moat)
- Utility-side data partnerships cannot be secured within 6 months (the data flywheel requires bilateral data, not just developer-side submissions)
- A well-resourced incumbent (Palantir, Microsoft, Wood Mackenzie) announces a directly competing product with existing utility relationships before GridMind reaches 20 active projects
